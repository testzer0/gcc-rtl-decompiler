\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A class representing call commands in the program. These are used to call other functions/subroutines. There are two kinds of calls, \mbox{\hyperlink{classRetCall}{Ret\+Call}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classNoRetCall}{No\+Ret\+Call}}.
